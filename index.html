About Me

Hey there!
My name is Tolu. I'm a developer and a data scientist with a background in chemical and biosystems engineering originally from Nigeria. Over the years, I have taken up analytics and engineering roles in various companies across multiple industrial sectors. The one thing all these roles have in common is that there are always problems to solve and boy do I love solving problems.
Whether it’s finding the most elegant way to write a line of code or figuring out which chord fits best into a progression, I love the challenge of finding a way. Maybe that’s why I love coding and the satisfaction that comes from finding that one algorithm; or why I study psychology and rationality in my spare time to figure out how people think. As long as there’s a problem to solve or a challenge to puzzle over, it’s bound to be something I love!
When I am not behind a keyboard or a reactor I enjoy watching anime, listening to music (country and soul) and reading books.
This site is a collection of my work and interests; if any of those projects intrigue you, do not hesitate to reach out. You can check out my GitHub repos for projects I’ve worked on. You can also reach me on LinkedIn or send me an email if you want to discuss job opportunities and/or collaborations. Thank you for visiting my webpage!
